Here are some of my Projects

Weather App

An application to check the weather in different cities. It is made with React and calls an API that provides the weather information. The form is validated and returns the current temperature with a maximum and minimum.

Breaking Bad Phrases App

This App was made in React. When you press the button, a phrase from the Breaking Bad series appears and it changes each time you press the button. This happens by calling an API.

Hairdresser Appointment App

This app was created with React. It contains form validation, adds the appointments in a column next to it, and they can then be removed. You can add as many appointments as you want and they are saved in the local storage, so if you refresh the page, no data is deleted.

Car Insurance App

This car insurance application is made with React. The result of the form is based on the information provided by the user. The form is validated. There is a spinner that simulates the loading time of an API call. It is designed with Styled Components.

Make Up Website

A young and fresh website made with Javascript for an audience that demands good photography and design. The website has a carrousel, photo gallery using modal, a parallax effect in the contact form background and an embedded video.

Weekly Budget

A budget control React Application. It has form validation that only accepts numbers as budget. In this application you define your budget, and then expenses can be entered. The expenses are shown in a list. The remaining amount is updated as expenses are being added, and it changes the color when you are reaching the limit as a warning to the user.

Turism Website One

This website was designed for a tourism company with an older audience in mind. Institutional colors, visual appeal and ease of use were taken into account. This website has two carrousels, one for pictures and the other one for client's opinion. It also has two parallax effects, one in the banner at the begining and the other one in the contact form background. Javascript was used for its construction.

Turism Website Two

This website was the second part presented to the tourism company. Institutional colors, visual appeal and ease of use were again taken into account but it was made by Bootstraap library. The original idea was to take it as an extension of the first page where you would see more trips and part of the blog with useful data. This part has a carrousel with images and text. The pictures of the places to visit are made in the style of Masonry Cards and a sticky navbar.
